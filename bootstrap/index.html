Hello, World!

Tweet @LetsTalkHashtag, and we'll tweet you back!

LetsTalkHashtag is a twitter bot that helps you make internet friends. Tweet us, throw in a hashtag, LetsTalkHashtag will automatically match you up with a fellow twitter user. It's that easy & fun!

Step One: Follow
Make sure you're following the twitter bot. Follow @twitterapi
Step Two: Tweet
Tweet us with a hashtag of whatever you have in mind. Tweet to @LetsTalkHashtag
Step Three: Make Friend
Now wait, you'll get a twitter notification when we find your hashtag soulmate. Enjoy!
